# OperaIQ — AI-Powered Quick Commerce Operational Intelligence System

## Overview
OperaIQ is an AI-powered operational intelligence platform designed for quick commerce and supply chain operations. The project integrates warehouse fulfillment analytics, dispatch intelligence, SLA monitoring, rider utilization analysis, and operational cost optimization into a unified operational analytics system.

The platform models the complete fulfillment lifecycle — from warehouse picking and staging to last-mile delivery — to identify operational bottlenecks, improve throughput, and optimize delivery economics.

---

## Problem Statement
Quick commerce operations involve multiple interconnected systems including warehouse operations, dispatch batching, rider allocation, inventory flow, and SLA management. Small inefficiencies in warehouse picking or dispatch staging can significantly impact customer experience, delivery speed, and operational cost.

OperaIQ aims to:
- Diagnose operational inefficiencies
- Improve fulfillment throughput
- Reduce SLA breaches
- Optimize last-mile delivery performance
- Build operational intelligence dashboards for decision-making

## Key Insights
- Warehouse pick latency strongly impacts downstream SLA adherence.
- Dispatch staging delays significantly increase operational costs.
- Peak-hour congestion creates major fulfillment bottlenecks.
